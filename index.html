Enter length, width, and wall height. There's no calculate button: the ticket updates live as you type, showing the buy number big and the exact need underneath.

QUESTIONS

THE FIVE THINGS EVERYONE ASKS.

Is it really a one-time purchase?

Yes. $4.99, once, and Pro is yours for good — Family Sharing included. There is no subscription tier, no renewal, and no plan to add one. It's the whole reason we put "No subscription. Ever." on a stamp.

What's free and what's Pro?

Free forever: all eleven calculators, unlimited rooms, and the Project Sheet with automatic buy totals. Pro adds the Home Binder and every export — email, CSV, pull sheets, and copy. You can plan an entire project without paying a cent; Pro is for taking it with you and keeping records.

Does it work offline?

Completely. Every calculator, the Project Sheet, and the Home Binder work with no connection — measure in the basement, the attic, or the far corner of the yard. You only need a connection to email a project.

Where does my data live?

On your device, and nowhere else. No account, no cloud sync, no analytics, no ads. Our App Store privacy label reads "Data Not Collected" — the strictest label Apple offers.

Is there an Android version?

Not yet — CastleWise is iPhone and iPad first. If you want it on Android, email us; we count the requests, and demand decides the roadmap.
