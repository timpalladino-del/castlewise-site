PRIVACY POLICY

WE COLLECT NO DATA.

Effective July 15, 2026 · Applies to the CastleWise iOS app and castlewise.app

The short version: CastleWise does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device. That's the whole policy — the rest of this page just says it more formally.

WHAT WE COLLECT

Nothing. CastleWise has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs that collect data. Our App Store privacy label is "Data Not Collected" — the strictest label Apple offers.

YOUR PROJECTS AND RECORDS

Calculations, saved rooms, project sheets, and Home Binder records are stored locally on your device only. We cannot see them, access them, or recover them. If you delete the app, that data is deleted with it. If you export or email a project, that file goes only where you send it.

PURCHASES

CastleWise Pro is a one-time purchase processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details. Apple's handling of that transaction is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

EMAIL YOU SEND US

If you email a feature idea or support question, we receive your email address and whatever you write — because that's how email works. We use it only to reply and to build the things you ask for. We don't add you to marketing lists, and we don't share it with anyone.

THIS WEBSITE

castlewise.app is a static page. It sets no cookies and runs no analytics or advertising scripts. Fonts are served by Google Fonts, which may log standard technical request data per Google's fonts privacy notes.

CHILDREN

CastleWise collects no data from anyone, including children. There is nothing further to disclose because there is nothing collected.

CHANGES

If this policy ever changes, we'll update this page and the effective date above. Given that the policy is "we collect nothing," we don't expect it to change.

CONTACT

Questions about privacy: features@castlewise.app
